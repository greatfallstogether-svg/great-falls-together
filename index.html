What we support

Common-sense improvements that keep Great Falls livable, safe, and connected.

Safe sidewalks & crossings

Fill gaps, improve crossings, and make walking safer—especially near schools and community destinations.

Connected trails & bike routes

Connect neighborhoods to the Village and parks with practical, family-friendly links.

Safety-driven transportation planning

Support improvements that reduce conflict points and make driving patterns safer and more predictable.

Access to everyday services

Help residents access essentials locally—without long trips for basic needs.

Practical improvements help neighbors of all ages move safely and confidently.
